About WiDS Kenya

WiDS Kenya is an independent event organized by Kenyan Ambassadors to coincide with the annual Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference held at Stanford University and an estimates 150+ locations worlwide. All genders are invited to attend WiDS regional events, which features outstanding women doing outstanding work.

WiDS Conference Kenya 2025

The WiDS 2025 Conference will take place at the Microsoft Offices in Dunhill Towers, focusing on women's brain health through the lens of data science. This year’s event will feature five inspiring speakers, presenting a wide range of topics within the field.
To gear up for the upcoming Kaggle-hosted datathon competition, we will conduct a series of datathon workshops between January and February 2025. These three workshops will be held in a hybrid format, offering the flexibility of attending in person at Microsoft or joining virtually.
