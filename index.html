About WiDS

WiDS Kenya is an independent event organized by Kenyan Ambasssadors to coincide with the annual Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference held at Stanford University and an estimates 150+ locations worlwide. All genders are invited to attend WiDS regional events, which features outstanding women doing outstanding work.

WiDS Annual Conference 2023
